    About NatureScope

    NatureScope is an interactive exploration tool for Wisconsin's amazing biodiversity. Our data comes from GBIF, a global network of naturalists, researchers, and citizen scientists documenting wildlife observations. This project helps communities understand and appreciate the natural world in their regions.

    Get Involved

    Want to contribute wildlife sightings? Visit GBIF.org to submit your observations. Help us build a comprehensive picture of Wisconsin's ecosystem!
